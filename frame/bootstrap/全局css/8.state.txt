
Class	描述
.active	鼠标悬停在行或单元格上时所设置的颜色
.success	标识成功或积极的动作
.info	标识普通的提示信息或动作
.warning	标识警告或需要用户注意
.danger	标识危险或潜在的带来负面影响的动作

<!-- On rows -->
<tr class="active">...</tr>
<tr class="success">...</tr>
<tr class="warning">...</tr>
<tr class="danger">...</tr>
<tr class="info">...</tr>

<!-- On cells (`td` or `th`) -->
<tr>
  <td class="active">...</td>
  <td class="success">...</td>
  <td class="warning">...</td>
  <td class="danger">...</td>
  <td class="info">...</td>
</tr>
